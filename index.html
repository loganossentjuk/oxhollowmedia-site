Vision

Ox Hollow strives to bring people closer to nature — illuminating stories of action, passion, and belief in conservation, cultivation, and care.

Mission

To illuminate the unseen, instill devotion, and craft stories of our innate natural connections.

About

Behind
the lens.

Ox Hollow Media is the work of a photographer drawn to wild places and the people who protect them. Every frame begins the same way — quietly, on foot, waiting for the light to reveal something worth remembering.

Rooted in the Pacific Northwest, the work moves between coastline, high country, and the faces in between — always in service of a closer, more devoted relationship with the natural world.
